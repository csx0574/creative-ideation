# Method 10 — Pataphysics (荒诞物理学)

**Source**: Alfred Jarry, 1893, *Gestes et opinions du docteur Faustroll, pataphysicien*。https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/%27Pataphysics

**Originator**: Alfred Jarry (法国作家, 1873-1907, 《愚比王》作者)

## What it is
The "science of imaginary solutions" — pataphysics studies the laws governing exceptions, and describes a universe supplement to the conventional one. It treats the *particular* as the universal and embraces the absurd as more truthful than the rational.

## Core ideas
- Exceptions are more revealing than rules
- Equivalence between the real and the imaginary (Ubu's principle: things are equivalent to their own dreams)
- The 'clinamen' — the slight swerve (Epicurus) — that makes things escape determinism
- 'Anthropomorphisms' (the quality that allows us to see ourselves in objects)
- 'The eternal return' applied to a single point

## How to use this method in creative-ideation
1. User is stuck because the problem is governed by a "law" they accept (best practices, norms, conventions)
2. Ask: "What if the exception *was* the law?"
3. Construct a universe where the absurd case is normal
4. Extract the design implications for the conventional universe

## Best for
- 故意制造反讽/荒诞美学
- 拒绝平庸/套路
- 艺术/游戏/品牌定位
- 写"看起来很怪但其实很对"的东西

## When NOT to use
- 严肃商业/技术问题 (用 TRIZ)
- 紧急交付
- 用户没准备好接受荒诞

## Famous practitioners
- Ubu Roi (Jarry 自己的剧)
- Situationist International (Guy Debord)
- 大量 Oulipo 成员
- J.G. Ballard, Flann O'Brien, Boris Vian

## Output shape
- **Conventional law**: [the rule that everyone follows]
- **Exception that is more revealing**: [the case that breaks it]
- **Universe where the exception is law**: [the constructed world]
- **What this implies for the conventional universe**: [the actual design move]
