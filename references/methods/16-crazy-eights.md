# Method 16 — Crazy Eights (疯狂 8 分钟)

**Source**: Google Ventures Design Sprint, 2010s, 改良自 Birgit Mager 教授的 "6-3-5 Brainwriting" (1968, 德国) → 进一步简化。

**Originator**: Jake Knapp (Google Ventures) — popularized in *Sprint* (2016) → 简化自 6-3-5 脑写

## What it is
A timed sketching method: fold a paper into 8 cells, 8 boxes, 8 minutes total. One idea per box, one minute per box. Volume + speed breaks self-censor.

## The move
1. Set timer for 8 minutes
2. Fold paper into 8 cells
3. In each cell, sketch (or write) one idea — *1 minute per cell*
4. Don't stop, don't edit, don't judge
5. After 8 minutes, you have 8 raw ideas
6. Vote/discuss with team

## How to use this method in creative-ideation
1. User is "freshly stuck" — has tried brainstorming and got nothing
2. Set a hard timebox: 8 ideas in 8 minutes
3. Force volume: even bad ideas count
4. Post-hoc filter; pre-hoc no filter
5. Often the 6th-8th idea (when you've given up on getting it right) is the best

## Best for
- "Brainstorm 失败了" (脑暴常见死法: 自我审查)
- 设计/UI 草图
- 命名/Tagline 候选
- 流程变体

## When NOT to use
- 决策类问题 (用 Pre-mortem)
- 需要深度而非广度
- 用户焦虑/疲惫 (时间压力会加重)

## Variant
**6-3-5**: 6 people, 3 ideas each, 5 minutes — pass the sheet. After 5 rounds, 108 ideas.

## Output shape
- **Idea 1** (0-1 min): [...]
- **Idea 2** (1-2 min): [...]
- ...
- **Idea 8** (7-8 min): [often the most interesting]
