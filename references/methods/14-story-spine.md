# Method 14 — Story Spine / Story Skeleton (故事骨架)

**Source**: Kenn Adams, 2003, "How to Improvise a Full-Length Play" (即兴戏剧框架); 数字化为 Pixar/filmmaking 模板。https://www.kennadams.com/

**Originator**: Kenn Adams (戏剧即兴表演) → Pixar 改良为 "Pixar Pitch" 模板

## What it is
A fixed narrative skeleton you fill in. The classic 8-beat version:

1. **Once upon a time...** (设置常态)
2. **Every day...** (日常重复)
3. **One day...** (扰动出现)
4. **Because of that...** (连锁反应)
5. **Because of that...** (连锁加深)
6. **Until finally...** (高潮)
7. **And ever since then...** (新常态)
8. **The moral is...** (教训)

## Variations
- **Pixar Pitch**: 6 sentences — "Once upon a time there was [X]. Every day, [Y]. One day, [Z]. Because of that, [W]. Because of that, [V]. Until finally, [U]."
- **Save the Cat beats**: 15 beats across 110 pages (Blake Snyder, 2005)
- **Hero's Journey**: 12 stages (Joseph Campbell, 1949, *The Hero with a Thousand Faces*)

## How to use this method in creative-ideation
1. User has content/feature/event/launch with no clear structure
2. Force it into the skeleton — *be specific about beats*
3. The skeleton's tension (status quo → disruption → resolution) almost always produces a usable frame
4. Beats that are hard to fill usually mean the *material* is missing — that itself is a finding

## Best for
- 写故事/博文/讲稿
- 用户引导流程设计
- 视频脚本
- 任何"我有一堆内容但不知道顺序"的场景

## When NOT to use
- 非叙事性内容 (数据/参数/参考)
- 严格科技论文
- 用户想要随机/反结构

## Output shape
For each beat:
- **Once upon a time**: [status quo]
- **Every day**: [habit]
- **One day**: [disruption]
- **Because of that**: [consequence 1]
- **Because of that**: [consequence 2]
- **Until finally**: [climax/resolution]
- **And ever since then**: [new normal]
- **Moral** (optional): [the takeaway]
