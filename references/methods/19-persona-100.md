# Method 19 — 100 Personas (一百个用户)

**Source**: Luke Szyrmer / 早期产品研究方法, 现代广泛应用。https://www.lukew.com/

**Originator**: 实务方法, 无单一发明人; 广泛用于产品/UX/营销

## What it is
List 100 specific users. Not "millennials" — "Wei, 32, 在上海做外贸, 家里有 6 岁女儿, 周末带女儿去商场, 用 iPhone 13 mini, 去年卖了 47 笔订单". The move from 5 personas to 100 forces specificity: you can no longer write generic answers.

## The move
1. Generate 100 personas
2. Each must have: name, age, occupation, geography, specific behaviors, one specific complaint/frustration
3. The first 20 are easy (stereotypes); 20-60 force you to think about edge cases; 60-100 force you to find *truly* different people
4. Then ask: "Of these 100, which 5 would I build for first?"

## How to use this method in creative-ideation
1. User is making something for "everyone" (warning sign)
2. Force 100 personas
3. Cluster them into 5-7 groups
4. Pick 1-2 to build for first
5. The discarded clusters become the "not for" set — sharper positioning

## Best for
- 产品定位
- 营销文案 audience
- 创业方向选择
- "我不知道给谁做"

## When NOT to use
- 工具/库/底层 (无直接用户)
- 时间紧 (30 个就够)
- 已经是单用户场景

## Variant
**3 personas of one person**: 在不同时刻 / 角色下, 一个人可以是多个 persona (家长/员工/患者/粉丝/...). For mapping products that serve a user across contexts.
